Profile: Xushan Li

Bachelor of Art in History

  • Place of Origin: Beijing, China
  • Age: 22
  • Gender: Female
  • Occupation: Grocery clerk, graphic artist, and student
  • Quote: “I can say things with colour and shape that I can't express in words”
  • Relationship Status: Single
  • Likes: Cats, Illustrator, and Staedtler Lumograph pencils
  • Dislikes: Poutine, Tim Hortons
  • Aspirations: Full-time employment as a graphic artist
  • Dependents: None
  • Enrollment Status: Part-time
  • Previous Education: High school
  • English Skills: Excellent spoken skills, but difficulty reading and writing
  • Learning Disability: Reading/writing. Has trouble comprehending books (there is a lot of reading). Struggled in high school (even in her native language), but is taking one course at a time and is very motivated.

Xushan moved to Canada (to Barrie specifically) with her family when she was sixteen years old. She generally has learning difficulties with reading comprehension, which is compounded by a language barrier. Xushan has always been interested in visual arts, and is taking Laurentian's Art History program (through Georgian) to develop a sound historical and theoretical foundation for what she hopes will be her field (Graphic Design), and to learn English better.
